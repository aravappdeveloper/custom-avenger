How to play:
Use arrow keys to move the character (Groot) around.
F = Face
B = Body
L = Legs
R = Right Hand
H = Left Hand
Shift + P = Increase Size
Shift + M = Decrease Size
*IF YOU MISPLACE A PART, CLICK ON IT ONCE, THEN DRAG IT TO WHERE YOU WANT TO MOVE IT*
